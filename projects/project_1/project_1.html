Dizajn.mk

Dizajn is a social media network with a blog-like interface designed for designers in Macedonia to share their knowledge of design, graphics, and software.

This project was created as part of my HTML/CSS course.

Wireframe

Wireframing is a crucial communication method for website design. It enables me to share my concepts and layouts with my client before proceeding to design the final design.

Both the low fidelity designs and the high fidelity wireframes are displayed here.

Unique Features

  • A modern design with simple navigation, filters for articles, and a portfolio.
  • Capability of setting up a user profile to share their work with clients and colleagues.
  • Having the option to upload both portfolios and articles, as well as allowing brands to advertise.
  • Filtering system to help viewers discover everything from articles, portfolios and freelancers.

User Testing

When building a website, user testing is crucial since it allows you to observe how actual users interact with your product.

Learning what they like and dislike can help us build better user experiences. Here is some feedback I've got from my users.

1

Likes the website's aesthetic and the concept of a social platform, but thinks that the logo doesn't fit the company's branding.

2

3

4
Emilija T.
